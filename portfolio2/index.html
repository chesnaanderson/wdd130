About Me

Welcome! I’m Tana. I’m a nurse practitioner and health content marketing writer and journalist here to help YOU.

High-quality written content creation, while absolutely imperative, can be time-consuming and laborious.  A collaboration with me will ease this burden of written content creation and embolden your organization to continue to attract and retain loyal consumers. I bring an expert, nurse practitioner written perspective to content writing that will speak directly to your readers and keep your organization’s mission at the forefront of their minds. I provide both B2B and B2C deliverables depending on your needs. My goal is to help you and your cause through whatever means of writing I can help with!

If you would like to collaborate with me, you can expect that I will…

  • Provide friendly customer service
  • Meet and respect all deadlines
  • Communicate with you at all stages of the content creation process
  • Individualize my writing services to meet YOUR unique needs
